Calculation Limitations

Our calculations are based on widely accepted formulas including:

  • Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
  • Harris-Benedict Formula
  • Katch-McArdle Formula

However, these formulas provide estimates only and may not be accurate for all individuals, particularly those with:

  • Medical conditions affecting metabolism
  • Extreme body compositions
  • Eating disorders
  • Pregnancy or nursing
  • Certain medications that affect metabolism

Metabolic rates can vary significantly between individuals due to factors such as:

  • Genetics
  • Medical conditions
  • Medications
  • Hormonal factors
  • Body composition
  • Previous dieting history

Weight Management Safety
